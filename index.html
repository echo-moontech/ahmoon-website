Anhui Moon Technology Co., Ltd.

Located in Bengbu City, Anhui Province, we are a scientific and technological enterprise integrating production, research and development, and sales. We are dedicated to providing stable product quality and efficient, high-quality services for global customers.

Our products are mainly sold to Southeast Asia, South America, Europe, and America, earning favor from users worldwide.
